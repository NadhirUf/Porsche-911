911 S/T

The Porsche 911 celebrated its 60th birthday in 2023 with a global series of events and celebrations. And one of the highlights of this special year for the legendary sportscar was the release of the new limited edition Porsche 911 S/T. The name is a nod to the special race versions of the 911 S produced from 1969 - internally called the 911 ST. The anniversary edition, which is based on the current 992 model, shares the same high-performance focus of its illustrious ancestor.
